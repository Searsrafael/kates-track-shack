About Our Tapes

The tapes that we put on the list, unless otherwise stated, are tested to make sure that they play, but have not been tuned-up (new pads/splices). These tapes can be tuned-up before they ship for an additional $2 per tape.

For those that like to do their own repairs, we also sell the repair supplies needed to do that. When you place your order for tapes, if you want the tapes tuned-up before they ship, please let us know.

Shipping & Insurance

We require delivery confirmation on all US packages. Most International packages usually have some sort of tracking number. This is for your benefit as well as ours.

Insurance is optional but highly recommended on larger packages that might tend to get damaged in shipping. We can't be responsible for packages that are lost, stolen or damaged in shipping unless the package is insured.
